Data Deletion Instructions

If you want to delete your data associated with our app, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Facebook account.
  2. Go to the settings section of your Facebook account.
  3. In the "Apps and Websites" section, find our app and click on it.
  4. Click on "Remove" to delete the app and all associated data.
  5. If you have any issues or need further assistance, please contact our support team at zubeidhendricks@gmail.com.

We are committed to protecting your data and ensuring your privacy. Thank you for using our app!
